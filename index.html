Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro, born 5 February 1985, plays for Spanish La Liga club Real Madrid and is the captain of the Portuguese national team.In 2009, Ronaldo became the most expensive footballer in history after moving from Manchester United to Real Madrid in a transfer worth £80 million (€93.9 million/$131.6 million). In 2012, Diego Maradona declared that Ronaldo was "the best player on the planet."

Cristiano Ronaldo scored 807 career goals, making him the most prolific goalscorer in the history of men's football. Lionel Messi is 48 goals behind Ronaldo, but you never know if he will catch up with him because of the difference of 3 years. The previous record holder was Josef Bican, who scored 805 goals between 1931 and 1955.
